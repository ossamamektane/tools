Martingale Loss Survival (2×)

Works with chip mixing.

Available chips: 10, 20, 50, 250, 1000, 5000 MAD
Usable base bets: any multiple of 10 MAD

1) Current Breakdown

Enter your bankroll and a base bet in multiples of 10 MAD.

2) Required Bankroll

Pick a base amount and how many losses in a row you want to survive.

3) What Base Can My Bankroll Handle?

Type your bankroll and how many losses in a row you want to survive. It picks the best usable base bet in steps of 10 MAD.

4) When Can I Bet More?

This shows the next higher base bets and the bankroll needed for each one.
